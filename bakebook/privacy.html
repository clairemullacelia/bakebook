Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

bakebook (“bakebook”, “we”, “us”) is a personal recipe development and storage app. This policy explains what information the app handles, why, and the choices you have. By using bakebook you agree to this policy.

Information we handle

Account information

When you create an account you provide an email address and password, or sign in with Google or Apple. If you use a social sign-in, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from that provider. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider (Google Firebase) and are never stored by us in readable form.

Content you create

bakebook stores the recipes you make and everything in them — names, descriptions, ingredients, method steps, notes, bake logs, categories, and any photos you add. This content is saved to your account in the cloud and cached on your device so the app works quickly and offline.

Technical information

Our infrastructure providers automatically process standard technical data (such as IP address, device/browser type, and timestamps) to operate the service securely. We do not use this for advertising.

Subscription information

If you subscribe to bakebook+, your payment is processed by Apple through the App Store — we never receive or store your card details. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status, so we know whether your account is active (premium) and can unlock the corresponding features. We also keep simple counts of your activity (for example, how many butter messages you’ve sent today) to apply fair-use limits.

The AI assistant (“butter”) and recipe import

When you chat with butter, or import a recipe from a link or photo, the relevant information — your message or the recipe content/image, along with the recipe context needed to answer — is sent to Anthropic (the makers of Claude) through our secure backend, so it can generate a response. Anthropic processes this to return the result. Per Anthropic’s API terms, this content is not used to train their models. Please avoid sending sensitive personal information to butter.

Who we share information with

We do not sell your information, show you ads, or use third-party advertising trackers. We rely on a small number of service providers to run bakebook:

  • Google Firebase — hosting, sign-in, database, and photo storage (privacy).
  • Anthropic — the AI that powers butter and recipe import (privacy).
  • Apple — processes bakebook+ subscription payments through the App Store (privacy).
  • RevenueCat — manages bakebook+ subscription status (privacy).

These providers process data on our behalf to deliver the app. Data may be processed in the United States.

Keeping and deleting your data

Your content stays in your account until you remove it. You can delete individual recipes and photos in the app at any time. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at bakebook26@gmail.com and we will remove it.

Security

Access to your recipes is restricted to your own account through security rules, and data is transmitted over encrypted connections. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

Children

bakebook is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll change the “Last updated” date above. Significant changes may be highlighted in the app.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email bakebook26@gmail.com.
